About Me

Hello! My name is Kim Anderson.

I am an active multi-disciplinary artist of the San Francisco Bay Area and recently received my BFA in Animation/illustration from San Jose State University. I am a designer, a painter, a pixel pusher, a curious cat, an animator, and a fan of TMI. Currently I specialize in graphic design and illustration, but I certainly have a hand in most artistic mediums and love it when I have the opportunity to cross-pollinate.
